About Us

About

Zoom Innovation is a graphics and signage company growing as one of the top performers in this field in Kerala. We are equipped with the most modern facilities to deliver high-quality work with zeal in graphics, signage and glass films. With excellent design, unique combinations of materials and technology.

Our services include the production and installation of various types of signage’s and graphics. We have a variety of options from which you can choose the correct choice fitting to your demand. We use Latest machineries and materials like, HP Latex, Eco Solvent, Lamination, Laser Cutting, Router, Chanel Bending Machine, and Liquid Acrylic to ensure you are getting the result you desire.

Our Milestone

Back in the year 2014, when we founded Zoom Innovation, we intended to realize several clients' brand signage needs. By doing so, we were not just establishing another concept development company in the city of Cochin. Instead, we wanted to bring those beautiful visions conceived in our clients' minds into a reality. From the beginning to this point, everyone at Zoom Innovation has worked hard to bring it to this excellence level. The ultimate judge for every business is its respective market. And Zoom Innovation has been able to satisfy our clients with our extreme dedication and high-quality performance up until now. Through our employees' pure talent and hard work, we have reached this point as a competitive branded signage company based in Cochin. Today, years after our founding, our satisfied customers stand as our witnesses. That is why our list of clientele includes many prominent and reputed business establishments like Infosys, Geojit BNP Paribas, Xerox, Vayalar Resorts, GMA, and KPMG.
